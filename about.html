Hi, we're the family behind Simple AI Kids 👋

Two parents, two curious kids, and one very opinionated baby-blue Quaker parrot who speaks English and Chinese — on a mission to help kids make with AI instead of just watching it happen.

Why we built this

We think AI is the biggest learning opportunity our kids will get — bigger than the calculator, bigger than the internet. But most kids experience AI as a magic black box: they ask a chatbot a question and an answer appears. That's fun, but it's not understanding.

We built this site because we wanted our own two kids — and yours — to make with AI instead of just consuming it. Train a model. Break a model. See what data does. Notice bias. Build something silly. Build something useful. When kids build, they stop being scared of AI and start being curious about it. That's the mindset we want to hand them.

We also believe this fits squarely inside STEM — the same muscles as math, science, engineering, and coding, just pointed at the most exciting tools of this decade. Every project here is hands-on, uses free tools, and is written in language kids can actually read.

Tiny, blue, and fully bilingual — speaks a little English and a little Chinese, and mostly uses both to demand snacks. Has delivered exactly zero useful code reviews, but squawks loudly the moment anyone starts recording a video. 10/10 would perch on your shoulder again.

Our approach

  • Everything is hands-on. No passive reading. Every project ends with something the kid has built or trained.
  • Free tools only. No paid accounts required. Works on a Chromebook.
  • Safety first. Grown-up notes on every project explain what data is collected, what stays local, and what needs an adult.
  • Honest about limits. We teach bias, overconfidence, and "this is just pattern matching" from day one.
  • Fun is not optional. If a project isn't fun for our kids, it doesn't go up.
