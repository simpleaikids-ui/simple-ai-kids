🎁 Lily's Prize Shop

Every finished project earns a sticker ⭐. Stickers are never spent — each prize just unlocks when your total reaches its number, so you can collect every single one. Jokes, rainbow confetti, secret phrases, a golden crown for Lily, and more!

How it works

  • Earn. Finish a project and tap "I Made It!" to earn a sticker.
  • Unlock. Each prize opens the moment your earned total reaches its number — no spending!
  • Collect them all. Because stickers stay forever, you can unlock every single prize on the list.
  • Site-wide. Once claimed, most prizes work across every page, every visit.
  • Private. Everything is saved on this device (localStorage). No account needed.
