Why This Site?

Most "AI for kids" material just explains. Here, kids build. Every project is something they can finish in one sitting with a Chromebook or laptop — no special hardware, no paid accounts, and no endless setup.

Projects are arranged by age, from playful drag-and-drop activities for little kids to real Python and API projects for teens. Each one lists exactly what you'll need, how long it takes, and what you'll learn.
